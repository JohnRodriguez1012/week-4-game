Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crstals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the number shown. You lose the game if your total go above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.

Good luck!
